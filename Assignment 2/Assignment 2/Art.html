A Good Girl's Guide to Murder

Author: Holly Jackson

Publication Date: 7 september 2019

$59.99

A Good Girl's Guide to Murder is a young adult mystery crime debut novel by Holly Jackson. The novel is the first in a series of three novels and one novella: A Good Girl's Guide to Murder; Good Girl, Bad Blood; As Good As Dead; and Kill Joy

About the Author

Holly Jackson is a British author of young adult mystery novels. She is best known for her A Good Girl's Guide to Murder series.
